About Me

Hi, I am a 4th-year Ph.D. candidate in Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Jiang Xu. Before that, I received my B.E. degree in Electronic Information Engineering from Zhejiang University in 2020. I have a broad interest in computer architecture, with a special focus on memory system. My research works have been published in various conferences and journals, including ICCAD, DATE, ASP-DAC, TPDS, etc. If you are interested in my works, feel free to drop me an email.
